FAQ

Common questions, answered plainly.

Is ProfMatch free?

Browsing, keyword search, and filters are free without an account. An account adds saved shortlists and personalized matching.

What does the match percentage mean?

A research-fit score — overlap between your interests and a professor’s recent, source-backed work. Not an admission chance.

Where does professor data come from?

Public faculty pages, personal/lab websites, and OpenAlex — each profile keeps source links and a confidence label.

Can I report incorrect data?

Yes. Every profile has a report path reviewed by admins before public data changes.
